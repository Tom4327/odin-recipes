Zucchini soup

Despite the bad picture you could have about soup and zucchini, this recipe is a beautiful journey in a farmers market on a sunny day

Very healthy, this recipe will help you cook zucchini to make it very tasty

Ingredients :

  • zucchini
  • butter
  • onions
  • parsley
  • basil
  • garlic

Steps:

  1. wash the zucchinis
  2. cut the zucchinis
  3. cook the onions and the garlic together in the butter
  4. add the zucchinis with the onions and garlic
  5. let it cook for 20 minutes
  6. grind everything together without forgetting the aromatics
